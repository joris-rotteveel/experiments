milkshake

Ingredients: 4 scoops chocolate ice cream

240ml ginger beer 90ml Baileys

5 pieces candied ginger

1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper Honey Chilli, cinnamon and sugar spice blend

Ginger-infused whipped cream

Candied ginger slices

To make:

Blend ice cream, ginger beer, Baileys, candied ginger pieces and cayenne pepper.

Rim a glass with honey, then dip in the spice blend.

Pour in milkshake, finish with whipped cream and candied ginger slices.

Fend off all the boys.
